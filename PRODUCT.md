# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Technically-minded readers close to the AI frontier: engineers, founders, researchers,
and sharp operators who think about agents, memory, context, retrieval, interfaces, and
how enterprises actually adopt new software. They arrive to *read and think*, not to be
sold to. Context is usually a focused desk session, wanting long-form reasoning without
glare or hype.

## Product Purpose

A personal field notebook and essay archive from someone trying to understand the AI
frontier properly, before the ideas get flattened into buzzwords. It is a living,
version-controlled wiki: part essay archive, part working notes, part proof-of-work.
Success is a reader thinking "this person is actually thinking about this," not "nice
personal brand." Depth, taste, restraint, curiosity. Proof over image.

## Brand Personality

Quiet frontier editorial. Three words: **sharp, grounded, curious.**
Voice: "I'm trying to understand this properly," never "here's why everyone is wrong."
Calm, structured, slightly intense, intelligent, warm. A clean study room with good
espresso, a notebook, and one serious question on the table. Reflective thinker plus
technical builder, held in tension: serious without performing seriousness.

## Anti-references

- **AI-influencer:** dark backgrounds, neon gradients, "agentic revolution," "10x." Hype.
- **McKinsey-lite:** whitepaper layout, vague diagrams, "unlocking value," "transformation."
- **Academic-only:** dense PDF aesthetic, formal abstracts, zero personality.
- **Personal-brand guru:** huge face, "I help organizations…", a marketing funnel.
- **SaaS landing page:** centered hero, identical icon-cards, gradient buttons.

## Design Principles

1. **Proof over image.** Show the thinking and the work; never perform polish for its own sake.
2. **Let the writing breathe.** Whitespace and reading comfort beat density and decoration.
3. **Restraint is the statement.** One warm accent, quiet type, no hype color or motion.
4. **Slightly ahead of the room.** Calm and intelligent, with just enough intensity to feel alive.
5. **Don't overbuild.** Keep the system simpler than the writing habit it serves.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA contrast on warm paper and warm dark. Respect `prefers-reduced-motion`
(motion is minimal by design). Readable measure (65–75ch). Keyboard-navigable,
visible focus states. Color is never the sole carrier of meaning.
