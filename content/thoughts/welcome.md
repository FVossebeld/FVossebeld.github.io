This is the **thoughts** area of the garden — where I push public thoughts and cool ideas as they come.

This first note is just a placeholder so you can see what a page looks like. To add your own:

1. Create a new `.md` file anywhere under `content/`.
2. Give it a `title` in the frontmatter (the block at the top between `---`).
3. Link to other pages with `[[double-bracket]]` wiki-links, e.g. [[about]] or [[how-this-works]].
4. Commit and push — the [[how-this-works|GitHub Action]] rebuilds the site automatically.

> [!tip] Drafts
> Add `draft: true` to a page's frontmatter and it won't be published, even while it lives in the repo. Handy for half-baked ideas.

Happy gardening. 🌱
